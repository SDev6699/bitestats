Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

Our extension collects data from the web pages you visit to provide the functionalities of the extension. The data is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Permissions and Justifications

Our extension requests the following permissions to function correctly:

  • activeTab: To interact with the current tab and extract necessary data.
  • cookies: To manage and retrieve cookies related to your Grubhub and DoorDash sessions for analysis purposes.
  • host permissions: To access the content of the web pages from Grubhub and DoorDash.
  • remote code use: To allow the extension to execute necessary scripts within the web pages you visit.
  • scripting: To enable the extension to inject scripts into web pages for data extraction.
  • storage: To store the collected data locally within your browser.
  • tabs: To access and manage your tabs to provide a seamless user experience.

Data Usage

The data collected is used solely for the purpose of providing the functionalities of the extension. We do not share your data with any third parties.

Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your data stored in your browser. However, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
